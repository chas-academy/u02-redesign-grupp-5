Resto Frit Wholesalers

Offering quality products at the best possible prices is the mission of Resto-Frit and Resto-Culinair. This brand brings together fifteen wholesalers who supply a total of hundreds of hospitality establishments. The members of Resto-Frit and Resto-Culinair all guarantee fast and personalized service. The members are all hospitality wholesalers who have a family history and approach. So many pragmatic companies who know exactly the real needs of the hospitality industry, and therefore want to provide tailor-made work. In terms of services and contacts, they will necessarily do better than the large impersonal chains. And it is precisely the collaboration that characterizes Resto-Frit and Resto-Culinair which allows members to offer quality products at extremely competitive prices. Resto-Culinair, the culinary little brother of Resto-Frit, emphasizes a partnership with establishments of a higher culinary level. Resto-Culinair provides both the friendly brasserie and the high-end restaurant. Do not hesitate to contact one of our wholesalers to find out more. Or find us at our annual show at Flanders Expo.

A Reputation Built On Customer Values

It has now been 25 years since Resto-Frit was founded. Over time, the group has become a key player in the sector and has built an excellent reputation. Year after year, a number of profitable concepts and marketing actions have been developed. The best known is undoubtedly the Smulllbox. A close collaboration with Studio 100 resulted in this practical case containing fried snacks and which has been boasting dizzying sales figures for years. Learn more about Resto Frit.
